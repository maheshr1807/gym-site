FORGE started as a single room with a barbell rack. Nine years later, it's Coimbatore's home for coached, honest strength training.

Our Story

From One Rack To A Full Studio

FORGE opened in 2017 in a 400 sq ft space with two squat racks and a promise: every member gets a real program, not just a punch card. What began as a strength club for local athletes has grown into a full-service fitness studio — without losing the coached, community feel that started it.

Today we run strength, conditioning, yoga, and recovery programs under one roof, with a trainer-to-member ratio that keeps every session personal.

M

Our Mission

To make coached, science-backed training accessible to everyone — regardless of where they're starting from — through honest programming and real accountability.

V

Our Vision

To be the strength studio Coimbatore trusts first — building a community where progress is measured in years, not just months.
